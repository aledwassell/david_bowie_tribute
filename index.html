A timeline of his life.

Born David Robert Jones on the 8th of January 1947, Known as David Bowie was an English singer, songwriter, actor and record producer.

  • 1947, Born, David Robert Jones.
  • 1962-67, He formed his first band at the age of 15.
  • 1968-71, Space oddity
  • 1972-73, Ziggy Stardust and the release of the album Aladdin Sane
  • 1976-79, David Bowie rocks out in Berlin quite a lot.
  • 1980-88, Bowie stars in the movie Labyrinth and dresses all weird like a rock wizard.
  • 1999-2012, He gets all neoclassicist
  • 2016, The Release of Black star on January 8th.
  • 2016, Bowie dies of liver cancer aged 69.
